Why Behavioral
Frameworks?

Most strategy tools are built around who the audience is. These frameworks are built around what happens when they have to decide. That's a different question — and it produces different answers.

  • Identity doesn't predict behavior. The same person acts differently depending on context, timing, and friction.
  • Messages don't fail because they're wrong. They fail because the conditions around them don't support action.
  • Diagnosis before intervention. The right fix depends on identifying what's actually blocking the behavior — not assuming it's the message.
