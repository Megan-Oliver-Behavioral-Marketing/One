Research Case Study

Survey Design & Audience Insight Development

Objective

Understand public perception, priorities, and behavioral drivers to inform communication and engagement strategy.

Methodology

  • Designed survey instruments to capture both quantitative trends and qualitative insight
  • Structured questions to uncover not just opinions, but decision influences
  • Analyzed responses to identify patterns across audience segments

Key Findings

  • Stated attitudes did not consistently translate into intended behavior
  • Barriers were often tied to trust, clarity, and perceived relevance
  • Messaging effectiveness depended heavily on context and framing

Strategic Application

  • Refined messaging to better align with audience priorities and concerns
  • Adjusted communication strategy to improve clarity and perceived relevance
  • Informed campaign direction using audience-derived insight rather than assumption
